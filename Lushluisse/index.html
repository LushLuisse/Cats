TYPES OF CATS

Persian Cat

Persian cats are known for their long fur, flat faces, and calm nature.

Siamese Cat

Siamese cats are vocal, social, and very affectionate.

Maine Coon

Maine Coons are large, friendly cats with fluffy tails.

Bengal Cat

Bengal cats have wild-looking spotted coats and high energy.

Ragdoll

Ragdolls are gentle, relaxed cats that love human company.

Sphynx

Sphynx cats are hairless, affectionate, and playful.

British Shorthair

British Shorthairs are calm, loyal, and very plush-looking.

Abyssinian

Abyssinians are active, curious cats with sleek coats.
